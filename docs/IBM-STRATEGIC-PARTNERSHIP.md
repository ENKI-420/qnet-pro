# IBM Cloud Strategic Partnership Plan

## Executive Summary

DNALang is positioned to become a major strategic asset within IBM's quantum computing ecosystem by providing an enterprise-grade quantum development platform that makes quantum computing accessible through biological programming metaphors. This partnership leverages IBM Cloud infrastructure, IBM Quantum hardware, and Watson AI to create a world-class quantum development experience.
